Straight answers before you buy.

Are there add-on licenses or extra fees for specific integrations?+

No. Every integration, Prism, Redfish, IPMI, SNMP, IPFIX/NetFlow, HYCU, Datadog forwarding, Grafana forwarding, is included in the per-core price. Pricing is per-core, full stop.

Can I combine models, for example self-hosted plus MaaS?+

Yes. Many customers run Self-Hosted in dev and non-prod and MaaS for production. We keep the tooling and data model consistent across environments.

How are Nutanix cores counted?+

Total licensed physical cores across your Nutanix clusters. Hyper-threading and logical cores are not counted. Volume discounts are available for 500+ cores.

Is there a minimum commitment?+

Monthly billing has no minimum term. Annual terms are available if you want pricing predictability and simpler procurement.

What if my cluster or core count changes?+

Counts are reconciled monthly. Scale up immediately; reductions take effect at the next billing cycle.

Do marketplace purchases include the full platform?+

Yes. The same product runs across all three paths. The differences are procurement, deployment, and support, not feature access.
