The short answers.

Does my data leave my network?+

No. Overwatch runs as a local appliance and keeps telemetry on-prem by default. Nothing is forwarded unless you explicitly enable it, and forwarding is toggled per stream so you control exactly what leaves.

How long does it take to deploy?+

You can deploy Overwatch into a single Nutanix cluster in under an hour. Self-hosted setup typically runs one to two hours; the fully managed turnkey path is three to five days.

Do I have to replace Datadog or Grafana?+

No. Overwatch sits in front as the Nutanix-aware collection and analysis layer, then forwards filtered, enriched signals to the platforms your team already operates. Pick Datadog, Grafana, or both.

Are integrations an extra cost?+

No. Every integration, Prism, Redfish, IPMI, SNMP, IPFIX/NetFlow, HYCU, and the output platforms, is included in the per-core price. There are no add-on licenses or per-integration fees.

What does it actually monitor?+

818+ Nutanix metrics across Prism Central and Prism Element, plus hardware via Redfish and IPMI, network devices and flow via SNMP and IPFIX, and backup posture via HYCU, all in one operator console.

How is it priced?+

$7 per core per month self-hosted, Monitoring as a Service for a managed path, or through your existing marketplace relationship. A 14-day trial needs no credit card.
