Nutanix monitoring vs native Prism.

Prism is essential. It just isn't the whole observability picture. This is what teams add when they need broader infrastructure visibility, longer-range reporting, hardware context, network data, backup state, and optional cloud forwarding.

Keep using it for what it does well.

Native Nutanix management workflows

Cluster admin, tasks, and operational actions: the management plane Nutanix designed.

Cluster administration

Upgrades, lifecycle, replication, snapshot policies handled at the source.

Core PE/PC health and inventory

Live state for the cluster, hosts, and VMs from inside the platform itself.

The signal that lives outside Prism.

Cross-domain visibility

Nutanix plus hardware, SNMP devices, backup, and flows tied to the same operating story.

Long-range dashboards + reports

Scheduled executive and operator reporting with health scoring on top.

Optional cloud forwarding

Selective export to Datadog, Grafana Cloud, or Coralogix when teams need it.

Coverage outside native scope

Redfish/IPMI, SNMP, config backup, and HYCU context that Prism does not carry.
