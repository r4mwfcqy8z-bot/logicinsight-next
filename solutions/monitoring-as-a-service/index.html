What teams ask before they buy.

Does the Overwatch appliance leave my network?+

No. The appliance runs entirely inside your environment. Logic Insight operates it via secured, audited access. Telemetry stays local unless you explicitly enable forwarding.

What if I already use Datadog or Grafana?+

We integrate with both. Overwatch enriches and filters Nutanix telemetry, then forwards to whichever destination you already operate.

Can I start with Essentials and upgrade later?+

Yes. Tier changes take effect on the next billing cycle. We retain your dashboards, baselines, and rules across upgrades.

What happens if I cancel?+

You keep the appliance and the data it has collected. Logic Insight stops day-to-day operations and you transition to Self-Hosted, or take a managed offboarding to another tool.

How is this different from just buying Datadog directly?+

Datadog is a great destination. We are the Nutanix-aware ingestion + analysis layer that gets the right signal there, with ML baselines and forecasting that generic agents do not provide.
